Fox Pirate

TokenDescriptionCost
Builds your Line. Forces player to change his character token on adjacent tiles to any character token from his hand. Brings 1 victory point to player.![costWolf]

Turtle Viking

TokenDescriptionCost
Builds your Line. Gives additional action to place character token for player and his opponent. Brings 1 victory point to player.![costWolf]

Cat Aristocrat

TokenDescriptionCost
Builds your Line. Victory points are triplet in lines, which contains Cat token. Brings 1 victory point to player.![costPig]

Frog Ninja

TokenDescriptionCost
Builds your Line. Each turn player may move Frog to any empty adjacent tile, before placing new character token. Brings 1 victory point to player(See possible variants on the example below).![costWolf]

Mouse Guard

TokenDescriptionCost
Builds your Line. After placing Mouse, player may swap it with his or opponents character token on adjacent tiles(See possible variants on the example below). Brings 1 victory point to player.![costWolf]

Example:

Snake Archer

TokenDescriptionCost
Builds your Line. After placing, opponents should change character token on your choice from Map to any character token from his hand (check the example of tokens, that can be changed below). Brings 1 victory point to player.![costWolf]

Example:

Chameleon Traitor

TokenDescriptionCost
Builds your or opponent's Line. When character token placed on adjacent tiles to Chameleon token, he changes his color accordingly. Brings 1 victory point to player or opponent.![costRat1]

Crow King

TokenDescriptionCost
Enemy's spy. Builds opponent`s Line. Player gets half of victory points rounded down, from line with Crow. Brings 1 victory point to opponent.![costRat2]

Mole Digger

TokenDescriptionCost
Enemy's spy. Builds opponent`s Line. After placing Mole, player should swap it with his or opponents character token on adjacent tiles(See possible variants on the example below). Brings 1 victory point to opponent.![costRat1]

Example:

Prairie Dog Fairy

TokenDescriptionCost
Breaks your Line and/or opponents Line. After placing Prairie Dog, player may swap two any characters on adjacent tiles. Does not bring victory points.![costRabbit]

Example:

Hippo Dancer

TokenDescriptionCost
Breaks your Line and/or opponents Line. You should put Hippo between two tiles. Till your next turn Hippo occupies this two tiles. Next turn player must move Hippo to one of occupied tiles. After that player put his character token as usually. Does not bring victory points.![costRabbit]
